Who are we?

From the very beginning, Tesla has been guided by its core principles, to accelerate and improve upon the efficiency of an eco-sustainable mode of transport by bringing electric cars to market. At Tesla, we believe that this requires a willingness to innovate, and foster new ideas that bring us into the future.
Tesla was founded by the Musk brothers, who believed that when it came to climate change and driving, no one should have to compromise.
With our new lineup for 2021, the Æ, Ø, and Å models, we're paying tribute to our norwegian heritage. Our great grandfathers were from Norway, so we feel it's only right to name our new cars after the three special letters in the Norwegian alphabet. The new Tesla lineup for 2021 brings never before seen cutting edge technology to modern electric cars, at various price points.

Meet the team!

Håvard Musk

Meet Håvard, born in 1961, and the first born of the three Musk brothers. At an early age, he clearly showed an interest in technology and cars. When he was two, he already knew how to read and write.

At seven, he knew three languages fluently, and was studying with the middle school students. He early showed his passion for music and was confirmed to have perfect pitch. As well as a successful entrepreneur, he’s also an excellent pianist.

When he was 14, he began his studies at Harvard University, graduating from high school with the highest grades. After graduating from Harvard with a PhD in marketing, he decided to pursue his love for cars with his two brothers, Jeslin and Nikolai. In the year 2000, when he was 39, he founded Tesla Motors together with his brothers.

Tesla turned out to be hugely successful and made Håvard and his brothers billionaires in the span of one year. As the head marketing specialist at Tesla, Håvard has contributed vastly to Tesla’s success.

Jeslin Musk

Meet Jeslin, born in 1975, and the youngest of the Musk brothers. As with his brothers, he too showed signs of high intelligence at an early age.

When he was 14, he got accepted into Mensa with a verified IQ of 165. The next year he started his studies in Mechanical engineering at the University of Toronto, finishing his PhD six years later.

After finishing his PhD, he went straight into work at various companies. Four years later he teamed up with his brothers, and they decided together to establish the company Tesla, focusing on electric cars. Jeslin is now working as the head engineer at Tesla and has over 10.000 employees working for him in various countries around the world.

Nikolai Musk

Meet Nikolai, born in 1966, and the second born of the Musk brothers. When he was about 3 years old, he demanded to be allowed to borrow every book at the local library. Though that wasn't an option, he took home a bunch of books that day.

Just eleven years later, he was accepted at the university of Oxford, where he earned doctorates in mathematics and computer science. He founded Tesla along with his two aspiring siblings, and now also directs a scientific initiative for eco sustainable energy.

At a 60 Minutes interview, when asked about if he was happy with his life and achievements, he said: "I think I would have more of a life as a burger flipper. Or, like, a cowboy, or something like that. But as it stands, even though I sit at a keyboard for most of my time, I really enjoy what I do. I have always wanted to help everyone since I was a kid, and I'm happy that I'm in a position where I can do exactly that.”
